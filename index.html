Why we're doing this

01

1 in 11 US Adults

1 in 11 U.S. adults are in need of eye glasses and contacts and cannot afford them.

02

1 Billion Globally

1 billion people have vision impairment that is yet to be addressed.

03

Inequality

2 out of 3 people in low income countries who need eyeglasses do not have access.

04

Environmental Impact

When improperly disposed of, contact lenses break down into smaller particles, affecting soil, water systems, and aquatic ecosystems.

Why did I start this?
It seems so unfair that a large portion of the global and US population doesnt have access to clear sight when the technology has been around for over 100 years. We're here to help as much as possible.
